Health Tips

💧 Drink 8 Glasses of Water Daily

Staying hydrated is the simplest yet most powerful health habit. Water keeps your kidneys, skin and digestion healthy. Start your morning with a glass of water.

🥗 Eat Fruits & Vegetables Daily

Include at least 1 fruit and 2 vegetables in your daily diet. Citrus fruits like lemon and orange boost Vitamin C, strengthen immunity and keep diseases away.

🚶 Walk 30 Minutes Every Day

Just 30 minutes of walking daily controls blood pressure, prevents weight gain and improves your mood. Morning or evening — make it a daily habit.
